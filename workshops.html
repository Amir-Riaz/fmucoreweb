02 · WORKSHOP PROGRAMME

LEARN BY
DOING

Seven specialised, hands-on sessions run across Days 1 and 2, with one continuing into the main conference day — each built to deliver practical training, not just theory.

DAY 1

RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

Covers study design, literature review, data collection, statistical analysis, and manuscript writing. Tailored for students beginning or early in their research journey.

DAY 1

BASIC LIFE SUPPORT (BLS)

Internationally aligned BLS training covering airway management, chest compressions, and use of an AED — delivered by certified instructors.

DAY 1

RADIOLOGY WORKSHOP

Hands-on interpretation of X-rays, CT scans, and basic imaging principles relevant to clinical practice.

DAY 2

POSTER MAKING

A practical guide to designing academic research posters — structure, visual hierarchy, data presentation, and common mistakes to avoid.

DAY 2

CPR WORKSHOP

Step-by-step CPR training for adults, children, and infants — a practical, simulation-based session with real-time feedback.

DAY 2

SUTURES WORKSHOP

Introduction to basic surgical suturing techniques using simulation models — ideal for clinical-year students.

DAY 3 · MAIN CONFERENCE DAY

PSYCHOLOGICAL FIRST AID

Evidence-based psychological first aid training for managing acute stress responses in clinical and community settings.
