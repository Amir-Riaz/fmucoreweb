EVENT OVERVIEW

A landmark
first, by design.

FMU CORE 2026 marks the first national-level research conference in Faisalabad Medical University's fifty-year history. It's designed to elevate Pakistan's medical research culture and build a lasting community of evidence-based practitioners — undergraduate and postgraduate students across medical and allied health sciences, from every university in the country.

HOSTING INSTITUTION
Punjab Medical College, Faisalabad Medical University

VISION & OBJECTIVES

Not a one-day event.
A pipeline.

FMU CORE takes students from training through mentorship, abstract submission, peer review, and conference presentation — ultimately building toward publication.

Establish FMU's first international-level research conference, open to every medical and allied health institution in Pakistan.

Create a community of researchers — undergraduate and postgraduate, medical and allied health — from across the country.

Train students in research methodology, clinical skills, and evidence-based practice through dedicated hands-on workshops.

Implement a rigorous two-level scientific review process that upholds academic integrity from submission to acceptance.

Recognise and reward research excellence through competitive prizes and certificates of achievement.

Expand FMU's national visibility through the active Ambassador Programme and official university invitations.

PROGRAMME STRUCTURE

Three days, one arc.

Workshops run across the first two days to maximise hands-on participation; scientific presentations and keynotes anchor the main conference day.

  • ●Basic Life Support (BLS) Workshop
