6.2 · TWO-LEVEL SCIENTIFIC REVIEW

Built to eliminate bias.

All submitted abstracts undergo a structured two-level review process designed to uphold scientific rigor.

LEVEL 1

STUDENT COMMITTEE

Senior research students trained in methodology

  • ●Adherence to submission guidelines
  • ●Methodological soundness
  • ●Study design validity
  • ●Statistical appropriateness
LEVEL 2

FACULTY COMMITTEE

Faculty with publications and research experience

  • ●Scientific novelty
  • ●Clinical relevance
  • ●Overall validity
  • ●Final acceptance decision
