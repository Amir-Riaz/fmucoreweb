HOW REGISTRATION WORKS

Pick a level,
pick a role.

Every package sits at the intersection of two choices: academic level — undergraduate or postgraduate — and participation role — observer or presenter. Undergraduate packages additionally offer a Standard and Premium tier.

OBSERVER
Attends all sessions, receives an e-certificate. No presentation required.
PRESENTER
Includes poster printing and a display stand, plus a physical certificate.
STANDARD vs PREMIUM
Undergraduate tiers — Premium adds a conference bag, diary, pen, and meal box.
AMBASSADOR FEE
Discounted rate available on select packages through campus ambassadors.

Standard & Premium.

Trainees & professionals.

IMPORTANT NOTE

All certificates issued by FMU CORE are CME accredited. Physical certificates are provided to every presenter, while observer certificates are issued according to the selected registration package.
