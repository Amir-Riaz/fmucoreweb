Before you begin

  • Complete every required field carefully and accurately — fields marked with an asterisk * are mandatory. Once submitted, your abstract cannot be edited, so please double-check every section first.
  • Provide a valid email address and an active WhatsApp number — your Registration ID, QR code, and all official correspondence will be sent there.
  • Your progress is saved automatically as you move between sections.
  • Review your details carefully before submitting — this action is final. Submit only once; a confirmation email will follow shortly.
  • Keep your abstract within the stated word limit and attach up to two figures if required. After review, you'll be notified of your assigned presentation track (Poster, Oral, or Observer).
  • A non-refundable processing fee of Rs. 200/- is required after submission to begin the initial two-step review of your abstract (Student Reviewer, followed by Faculty Reviewer).
