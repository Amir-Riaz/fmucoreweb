01 · SCIENTIFIC COMPONENTS

WHERE THE
RESEARCH SPEAKS

Three formats carry the scientific programme on Day 3: posters open to every partner university, competitive oral presentations judged by faculty, and keynotes from national leaders in evidence-based medicine.

4.1

POSTER
PRESENTATIONS

Open to students from all universities across Punjab, accessed through FMU CORE's active Ambassador Programme. Ambassadors at partner universities promote the conference, facilitate registrations, and drive participation; official invitations are also extended directly to every partner institution.

ELIGIBILITY

Undergraduate & postgraduate students, medical and allied health sciences

SUBMISSION

Online abstract portal, live from 20 June 2026

REVIEW

Two-level scientific committee evaluation

PRIZES AWARDED FOR BEST POSTER PRESENTATIONS

4.2

ORAL
PRESENTATIONS

A competitive platform for researchers to present their work before a panel of faculty reviewers. Accepted abstracts are shortlisted based on scientific merit, then judged in a live Q&A format.

FORMAT

Competitive presentation with faculty Q&A panel

JUDGED ON

Content, delivery, and methodological rigor

PRIZES FOR TOP ORAL PRESENTATIONS

4.3

KEYNOTE
SPEAKERS

Distinguished national-level academics and clinicians, invited directly by FMU faculty, covering emerging research areas, evidence-based medicine, and the future of Pakistani healthcare.

4–6

ADDRESSES PLANNED

SPEAKER SELECTION

Chosen based on academic credentials and research impact — sessions open to all conference attendees
